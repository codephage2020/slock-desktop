Why Slock Desktop

Everything you need, in one desktop app.

No browser needed

Slock Web loads directly in a dedicated macOS window. No tabs, no URL bar — just your workspace.

One-click daemon control

Start, stop, and monitor your local daemon without touching the terminal. The app handles the lifecycle for you.

Themes, language, and auto-updates

Customize appearance, switch language, manage servers, and get the latest release — all from the app.
